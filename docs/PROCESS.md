# In Three Words — Process

## Weekly Selection Model

One painting per week. Entries are free. One person is selected at random each Sunday.

## For the Entrant

1. **Submit your entry** via the website or Instagram link. Provide your name, phone number, three words (nouns preferred), and preferred size. Free to enter.
2. **Wait for selection.** One entry is drawn at random each Sunday.
3. **If selected, you'll get a text.** You have 3 hours to confirm and pay ($20 or $25 depending on size) via Venmo or PayPal. If you don't confirm in time, the next name is drawn.
4. **Receive a photo** of the finished painting along with shipping confirmation.
5. **Get featured** on social media within 24–48 hours (First name, Last initial, City/State).

## For the Artist

1. **Collect entries all week.** Entries close Saturday night.
2. **Draw a name Sunday morning.** Text the selected person. If no response in 3 hours, draw again.
3. **Confirm payment.** Once payment is received, begin painting.
4. **Paint the piece.** Interpret the three words however you see fit. Full creative freedom.
5. **Photograph and ship.** Send the buyer a photo, then pack and ship.
6. **Post the reveal.** Share on Instagram/website within 24–48 hours with the buyer's name and city.
7. **Update the website banner** with the current week's selected person.
8. **Donate to the charity of the week.**

## Weekly Rhythm

| Day | What Happens |
|-----|-------------|
| Monday | New entry window opens. Update site banner with last week's completed painting. |
| Mon–Sat | Entries come in. Mid-week WIP tease on socials. |
| Saturday night | Entries close for the week. |
| Sunday morning | Draw a name. Text the selected person. |
| Sunday (within 3 hrs) | Selected person confirms and pays. If not, draw again. |
| Mon–Fri (next week) | Paint, photograph, pack, ship. |
| Weekend | Reveal post. Charity donation. Cycle restarts. |

## Shipping Guidelines

- **US only** at launch
- Use **Pirate Ship** for discounted USPS rates
- Packaging: cardboard corner protectors, cellophane wrap, rigid mailers for smaller pieces, flat cardboard boxes for mid-size

## Capacity

- **One painting per week** — this creates scarcity and keeps the workload sustainable
- Entries don't roll over — submit fresh each week
- The number of entries itself becomes social proof ("47 entries this week!")

## Charity Rotation

- Pick a new charity each week (or every two weeks)
- Post the charity alongside the painting reveal
- Over time, consider letting followers vote on the next charity
