The Process

Five steps. One painting per week.

Every painting starts with three words from a stranger and ends with original art on your wall.

01

Enter your three words

Submit your name, phone number, three words, and preferred size. Entries are open all week — free to enter.

02

One person is selected

Every Sunday, one entry is chosen at random. You'll get a text — you have 3 hours to confirm and pay.

03

I paint your piece

Original acrylic on canvas. I interpret your three words however they move me. Full creative freedom.

04

You get the original

Photographed, carefully packed, and shipped to your door. This is the only copy that will ever exist.

05

A charity benefits

A portion of every painting goes to a rotating charity of the week. Your art helps someone else too.

Questions

Good questions

Does it cost anything to enter?

No. Entry is completely free. You only pay if you're selected. If chosen, you'll get a text with payment instructions — you have 3 hours to confirm before the next person is picked.

What kind of words should I pick?

Nouns tend to work best, but there are no hard rules. Pick three words that mean something to you, or pick three completely random ones. The painting is my interpretation — that's what makes each one unique.

How is the selection made?

One entry is chosen at random each Sunday. Everyone has the same chance. If the selected person doesn't confirm within 3 hours, the next name is drawn.

Do I get to approve the painting first?

I'll send you a photo when it's finished, before I ship. But part of the magic is trusting the process. You give me the words, I give you art.

Is this really the original painting?

Yes. You receive the one and only original. There are no prints, no copies. Once it's yours, it's yours.

Can I enter every week?

Absolutely. If you're not selected one week, you're welcome to submit again the next. New entries each week — your words can be different every time.

Which charity does the donation go to?

I rotate charities on a weekly basis and announce each one alongside the painting reveal on social media. Follow along to see where your contribution goes.

Do you ship outside the US?

Not yet — US only for now. International shipping is on the roadmap as things grow.
